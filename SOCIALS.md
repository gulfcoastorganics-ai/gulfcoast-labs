# Socials

## Primary Channels

- GitHub: repository and build proof
- LinkedIn: outreach and credibility
- X: short-form project updates and signal

## Use Cases

- Share case study launches
- Announce new service openings
- Point people toward the contact page for scoped inquiries

## Rules

- Keep posts short and specific
- Lead with proof, not hype
- Link to the relevant case study or service page when possible

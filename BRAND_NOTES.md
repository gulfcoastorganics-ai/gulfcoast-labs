# Brand Notes

## Voice

- Direct
- Technical
- Calm
- Confident
- No hype language

## Visual System

- Dark base with cyan and steel accents
- Subtle grid/noise texture
- Glass surfaces with restrained borders
- Strong spacing and high contrast
- Minimal motion, no heavy animation stack

## Usage Rules

- GulfCoast Labs is the parent operator brand
- Client work must be labeled clearly
- Owned brands should be grouped as labs or portfolio
- Keep surfaces premium and sparse

## Typography

- Use a compact, modern sans stack
- Prefer crisp weights and generous tracking for labels
- Avoid playful or childish display treatment

# Service Offerings

## Core Offers

### Launch Prototype

- Starting at `$1,500+`
- Best for quick validation, a landing page, or a compact prototype
- Focuses on speed, clarity, and a clean handoff

### Operational Buildout

- Starting at `$5,000+`
- Best for a fuller system that needs structure and operational rigor
- Includes design, implementation, and delivery support

### Custom Systems

- Quote-based
- Best for larger or more technical builds
- Used when scope includes integration work, automation, or tighter security requirements

## Service Areas

- startup launch systems
- infrastructure-style websites
- AppSec dashboards
- AI tooling prototypes
- client review/handoff systems
- deployment pipelines

## Delivery Process

1. discovery
2. prototype
3. deploy
4. harden
5. handoff

## Positioning

The site should feel like a studio and an operator desk at the same time: compact, credible, and engineered to move quickly.

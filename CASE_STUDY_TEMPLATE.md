# Case Study Template

## Title

Project name and ownership label.

## Summary

One or two sentences on what the project is and why it matters.

## Challenge

Describe the problem or constraint that shaped the work.

## Before

- What was weak, missing, or unclear
- What the audience saw before the work

## After

- What improved
- What is clearer now

## Approach

- What was built
- Why the structure was chosen
- How the system was kept lightweight

## Operational Stack

- Surface
- System
- Delivery

## Deliverables

- Specific assets or outputs that the client received

## Outcome

One concise statement about the result or business value.

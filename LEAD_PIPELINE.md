# Lead Pipeline

## Stages

1. New inquiry
2. Qualified
3. Discovery booked
4. Scope agreed
5. Proposal sent
6. Active engagement
7. Delivered
8. Follow-up / referral

## Qualification Signals

- Clear project scope
- Budget fits the offer
- Ownership is understood
- The work is operationally useful

## Notes

- Keep the pipeline lightweight
- Capture inquiries quickly
- Separate client work from owned projects
- Preserve contact history for follow-up

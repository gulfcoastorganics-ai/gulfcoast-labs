# Outreach Plan

## Objective

Turn GulfCoast Labs into a trusted, outreach-ready operator studio.

## Weekly Cadence

- Publish or refresh one proof asset
- Send a short outreach batch to relevant founders or operators
- Follow up on open leads
- Revisit one case study or service page for clarity

## Messaging Priorities

- Clear ownership context
- Fast response expectations
- Premium but lightweight delivery
- Strong reasons to trust the process

## Targets

- startup founders
- security and infrastructure buyers
- operators who need a clean client-facing system
- brands that need a more established digital presence

## Follow-Up Rule

Follow up once with a short, specific note that references the relevant project or service.
